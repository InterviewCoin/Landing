How InterviewCoin Works

1. Define & Post Task

Companies define an interview task (for example, initial screening call), assign an NTV (InterviewCoin) amount, and schedule it with their selected candidate.

2. Apply & Complete

The candidate receives the details and completes the assigned interview task, demonstrating their skills.

3. Evaluate & Reward

Upon completion, the company sends the agreed-upon NTV (InterviewCoin) to the candidate's wallet.

4. Hire with Confidence

Companies assess the completed task for their hiring decisions, benefiting from a more engaged process. Candidates are fairly compensated for their work.
